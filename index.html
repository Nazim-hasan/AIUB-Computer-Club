ABOUT

AIUB Computer Club

The journey of AIUB Computer Club (ACC) started on 13th September 2012 with authorization from the Office of Student Affairs (OSA) of AIUB. Realizing the key importance to adapt along with upgrading technology, six chief founding members introduced this club with its mission and vision all set. The goal was to build up a strong community of AIUB students by offering them a potential platform that deals with technology and its advancement. AIUB Computer Club holds a high status as one of the most inspiring and admiring clubs in American International University-Bangladesh.
